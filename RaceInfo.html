Terms and Conditions:

  1. To enjoy student/staff rates, promo code is required.
    a) For NUS full-time students, please enter promo code: NUSSTUDENTXXXX (Last 4 digits of your student matriculation number).
    b) For NUS full-time staff, please enter promo code: NUSSTAFFXXXXXX (6 digits of your staff number).
  2. The promo code is not transferable and cannot be used to register for non-alumni/students/staff.
  3. PAssion card membership is non-transferable. To enjoy PAssion Cardholder rates, enter your 16-digit PAssion Card number (located at the back of the card) or PAssion POSB debit card number (located at the front of the card) in the Promo Code field upon registration.
  4. Team Category
    a) Team category participants are only eligible for the team prizes and will not qualify for individual categories.

Age Verification:

For the purpose of age verification, the age of the participant shall be taken as at 31 December 2022.

  • Participants for the 5KM Fun Run must be 6 years old and above as at 31 December 2022.
  • Child Participant for the 5KM Fun Run (family package) must be 6-12 years old as at 31 December 2022.
  • Participants for all competitive runs must be 16 years old and above as at 31 December 2022.
